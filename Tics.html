  • Tics

    Hyperkinetic brief and sudden movements that usually look like a voluntary movement, are prede by a premonitory urge and follow by a relief.

    More prevalent in males (4:1)

    Follow a rostro-caudal distribution

    Usually start in school years, peak at 12 and then improve by adolescence

    • Coomorbidity
      • OCD
      • ADHD
