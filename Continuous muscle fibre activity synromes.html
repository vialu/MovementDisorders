  • Continuous muscle fibre activity synromes
    • Definition

      Continuous electrical activity and contraction of muscle fibres that persist even on attempt of relaxation. It differs from dystonia in that the later condition shows more spontaneous fluctuations in muscle activity, with periods of relaxation in which there is EMG silence.

    • Clasification
      • Central

        Impulses coming from above the motorneuron level.

        They are abolish by sleep, general, spinal and local anesthesia

In myotonia, the activity arises from the muscle, in stiff person, the activity arises centrally.
