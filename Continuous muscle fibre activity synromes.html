Continuous muscle fibre activity synromes
Definition

Continuous electrical activity and contraction of muscle fibres that persist even on attempt of relaxation. It differs from dystonia in that the later condition shows more spontaneus fluctuations in muscle activity, with periods of relaxation in which there is EMG silence.
