      • Symptoms
        • lightheadness
        • paliptations
        • dyspnea
        • tremulousness
      • Affects predominantly women in childbearing age
      • Treatment
        • Non pharmacologic
          • Aerobic Excersice
          • Increase salt and water intake (10-12 gr of NaCl, 2-3 L of water)
          • Compress garmets
          • Improve sleep hygine
        • Pharmacologic
          • Blood volume expanders
            • Fludrocortisone 0.1-0.2 mg/day. (Monitor for hypokalemia)
            • Desmopresine 0.1 - 0.2 mg/day (monitor hyponatremia)
          • Heart rate inhibitors
            • propanolol (10-20mg up to 4 times a day)
          • Vasoconstrictors
            • Midrinona 2.5 - 15 mg
            • metilphenidate 10mg 2-3 times a day
          • Sympaticolytic
            • Clonidine 0.1-0.2 mg/day
